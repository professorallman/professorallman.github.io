Deserters
Marcus was the last great leader of the rebel army.  He was the oldest and wisest of the generals that reside in Jericho. Jericho was the rebel capital of the new word .It was assembled from pieces of the past. Great monuments were erected everywhere, tacked together by miss match pieces, resembling old Gods. Everything was rust; there was no other color in the city. The skies were grayed out, covered in enormous acid clouds. The rebels had a truce with the confederation during Passover, and it was the last day of Passover. Marcus gazed out into the city streets illuminated by wax candles. He could see sickly children running around looking for scraps to eat.

He turned around to continue speaking to Chancellor Leyon. “We just don't have the resources to continue on like this"

"Marcus this is our home! To give it up would be to join the confederation!"

"And to send my men out would be murder!"

"You have your orders; I think it wise for you to take your leave now" and at these words from the chancellor Marcus exited his chamber.

Sunrise came quickly, followed by gunshots. Marcus and his men set out of Jericho immediately. He tried with all his might to bring himself to carry out his orders, but he could not have his men slaughtered. He turned them around led them the opposite way of the battlefield. He looked back as Jericho fell. He had deserted his city to save only a few men. He could not have saved then all, could he?  They were no longer rebels, they were deserters.
